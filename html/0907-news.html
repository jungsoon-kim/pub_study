Fishing trip turns to disaster killing at least 10

At least 10 people died and an unknown number remain unaccounted for after a chartered fishing boat capsized off the country’s southern coast near the resort island of Jeju, maritime authorities said Sunday. As of press time, three people were rescued and transported to nearby Jeju Halla Hospital’s intensive care unit for hypothermia, according to local authorities. Their injuries are reportedly not life-threatening.
